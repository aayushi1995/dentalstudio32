Digital Smile Design (DSD)?

“…But above all, the DSD seeks to present to the public a new face of dentistry, more humanistic, emotional and artistic, further enhancing our noble profession before the society, because after all there are not many things in life more important than a healthy, natural, confident and beautiful smile.”

Dr. Christian Coachman, Founder of DSD concept

Nowadays having a mouth free of dental problems is not satisfying for esthetically driven patients. They wish to own naturally beautiful smiles that harmonise with their physical characteristics and most importantly with their personalities.

The concept of DSD is based upon the analysis of the patient’s facial and dental proportions, utilizing a series of high quality digital photographs and videos.

Analysing them provides an in-depth understanding of the relationship between teeth, gums, lips and smile with the facial features in motion and with emotions.

Digital drawings are easily made on the pictures to precisely communicate with the dental team when designing the smile, resulting in a clear, attractive and understandable treatment presentation for patients. The interaction between the patient and the dental specialist is improved by photos and videos taken at several steps of the treatment. The experiences all over the world have been tremendous

DSD ethically involves the patients in the restorative or smile enhancement process, making them the ‘co-designer’ of their own treatment by sharing objectives, expressing their desires and expectations with the restorative team. Once the restorative technical requirements are combined with the desires and emotional needs of the patient, great results will be achieved -creating a path to a natural, confident and beautiful smile.

DSD dentists are aware of all the contributing factors that are necessary to make patients more satisfied beyond conventional dentistry. Developing an artistic vision and a set of appropriate skills are needed in order to become a Digital Smile Designer. Each customised patient design can be clearly visualised and improved with the patient’s ideas. Therefore the DSD Concept allows dentists to implement these benefits for patients, creating a smile that reflects their own personality and greatly enhances the experience and the results for all.
